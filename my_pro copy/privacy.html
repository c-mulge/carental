Privacy Policy

At CaRental, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring that your experience on our website is safe and enjoyable. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Payment information for transactions (credit/debit card details).
  • Data related to your interactions with our website (IP address, device details, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our car rental services.
  • Process transactions securely.
  • Send promotional updates, offers, and relevant notifications.
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties only in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.
  • With trusted partners to facilitate our services (e.g., payment processors).
  • If necessary to protect the rights, safety, and property of CaRental or its users.

4. Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of online transmission is entirely secure.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand how you interact with our website.

You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer, though this may affect your experience on our website.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.
  • Request information about how we use your data.
